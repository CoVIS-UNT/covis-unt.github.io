Expectations of Graduate Students

in the Computer Vision and Intelligent Systems Lab

version 20250818

 

1.      All full-time graduate students are expected to work in the lab from 9 AM to 5 PM every weekday.

2.      Every student is expected to attend the weekly lab meetings.

3.      Exceptions for items 1 and 2 include holidays when the university is closed, job duties (e.g., TA office hours), and personal reasons (e.g., medical conditions and family issues).

4.      The presenter of a lab meeting should set up the presentation five minutes before the start of the meeting/presentation.

5.      Every student must have a clear goal and plan to reach that goal in each semester and summer break, and a weekly update of the progress is required.

6.      For the Ph.D. students in their first year, literature surveys and summaries of the readings are expected.

7.      For the Ph.D. students in their second year and after, at least one manuscript for submission to journals or conferences is expected.
