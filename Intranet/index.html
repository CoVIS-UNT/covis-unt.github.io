Student Travel Fund The department has a budget for funding student travel to conferences. Each student can request this funding at most once per year. Receiving this funding requires that students fill out the budget authorization form, which requests $750 from the College of Engineering, and request $500 from the Graduate School. These requests should be submitted to the department as early as possible.
