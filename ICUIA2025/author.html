Submission

Prospective authors are encouraged to contribute high-quality original papers to this conference. All submitted papers must be unpublished and not under consideration elsewhere for publication. All papers are limited to 12 pages and must be submitted at the ICUIA portal or click the following button
The final version will be accepted at the same portal.

The submitted papers will undergo a thorough review process. Each paper will be refereed by at least three experts in the field based on relevance, technical merits, originality, significance, quality and clarity. At least one of the authors will be required to register and present the paper in order to include the paper in the conference proceedings. Authors of selected papers will be invited to submit an extended and improved version to special issue in journals.
